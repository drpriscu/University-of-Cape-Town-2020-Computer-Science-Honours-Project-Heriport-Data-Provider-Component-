Title

An account of the rain's things or !khoa ka ||kerri-ssi !kau

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The rain's 'things' (or !khoa ka ||kerri-ssi !kau) of |han≠kass'o's country have large bodies and dwell in water; some of them kill people when they bite them.
