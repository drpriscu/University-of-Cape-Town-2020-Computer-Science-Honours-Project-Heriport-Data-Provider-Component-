Title

Making fire with two pieces of sticks; and tinder-making

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Making fire with two pieces of stick. By /han≠kass’o (L VIII. -28. 8471 and 8472). The people make fire (when they have no tinderbox) using two pieces of stick (from a divided arrow). The tree used for tinder is a dry Doornboom (thorn tree) that lies dead on the ground.

Comments
