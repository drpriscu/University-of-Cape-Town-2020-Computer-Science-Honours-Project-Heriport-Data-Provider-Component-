Life after death

Title

Life after death

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The names of those who are dead must not be uttered by the children at night. (L VIII.-26. 8310–8312). According to |han≠kass'o's grandfather (Tsatsi), when people die they become spirit-people. If the names of the dead (the old people) are called as if they are still alive, they will come and harm the living.

Comments

1) p.8309v: |hankass'o heard this from his grandfather (Tsatsi), 2) This story is found in Book VIII-26
