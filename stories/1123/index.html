Title

Words and sentences: at the Museum, 24 September 1880 (!nauxa and !khannumup)

Collection

Summary

!nauxa and !khannumup (at the South African Museum) give words and sentences in |xam (on the recto pages) and in !Ora ('Hottentot') on the opposite pages. These are largely the names of plants and animals and some descriptions of their habits or appearance.
