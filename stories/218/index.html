The Mantis, his wife and their things

Title

The Mantis, his wife and their things

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

... an explanation that the Moon has the power of talking, because he belongs to the Mantis, all of whose things talk (L II.-6. 670-677). |kaggen (the Mantis) speaks to the Moon because he has created the Moon from his shoe, which can speak. All of |kaggen's things can speak and have 'understanding', as do those belonging to his wife, |huntu !kat !katten.

Comments

1) p.669v: a note by ||kabbo on death; see //kabbo tells of death, the hole, and the path of the First Bushmen (pp.669v-670v), 2) This story is found in Book II-6
