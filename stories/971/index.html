The song of the Ngogan-a (a little bird)

Title

The song of the Ngogan-a (a little bird)

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

The song of the Ngogan-a, which is a little bird. The first line says it has gone away but the rest has not been translated.
