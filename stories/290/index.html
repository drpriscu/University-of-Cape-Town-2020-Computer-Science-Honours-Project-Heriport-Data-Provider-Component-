The death of ||kabbo's brother and sister-in-law

Title

The death of ||kabbo's brother and sister-in-law

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

||kabbo's brother was murdered because he stayed away from his wife and children and did not feed them. ||kabbo's sister-in-law later died of an 'internal sickness'. Her surviving sister took the orphaned child to ||kabbo, who raised her as his own child. ||kabbo journeyed to fetch the orphan from the dead woman's sick sister and met her halfway, as they lived far apart.
