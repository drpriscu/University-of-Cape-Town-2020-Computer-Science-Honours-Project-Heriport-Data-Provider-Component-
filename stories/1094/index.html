What the man says while cleansing himself

Title

What the man says while cleansing himself

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

The man who is cleansing himself (after shooting and killing another man) takes |kui fruit in his hands and speaks, saying that his heart must be calm and does not burn. The father-in-law says words that are intended for the relations of the man who has been killed.

Comments

1) See also Purification after shooting a person and The manner in which a man who is being cleansed, is prepared for drinking water and Further particulars regarding the purification or cleansing (|koa), 2) This story is found in Book XI & XII-11
