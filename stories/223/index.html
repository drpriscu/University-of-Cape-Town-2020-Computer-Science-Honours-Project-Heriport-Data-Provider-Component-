Flat Bushmen's' poison

Title

Flat Bushmen's' poison

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The |xam smell the poison which is warm and burns their nostrils. 'Grass Bushmen's' poison is a golden colour, 'Flat Bushmen's' poison is from a thorny bush or tree with a milky juice, and 'Flat Bushmen' also use snake venom as poison.
