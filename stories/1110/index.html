Title

Words and sentences: |uma at Mowbray, May 1880

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

|uma provides words and sentences at Mowbray in May 1880. These include parts of the body, some biographical information, things eaten in his country and the names and some descriptions of various plants.

Comments
