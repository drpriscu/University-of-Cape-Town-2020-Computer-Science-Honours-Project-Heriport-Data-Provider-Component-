Distribution of porcupine meat

Title

Distribution of porcupine meat

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Various parts of the porcupine are given to specific people in the group, for instance the wife of the man who killed the porcupine eats its tail. The hunter and the children eat its shoulder meat.

1) See The little porcupine, 2) This story is found in Book VIII-16
