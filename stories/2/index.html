Title

Words and sentences (given by Adam Kleinhardt)

Collection

Wilhelm Bleek notebooks

Summary

Words and sentences given by Adam Kleinhardt.

Comments

1) This story contains many notes on the verso pages concerning names of |xam, their genealogies, the names of the places they come from and the names of different groups of |xam (for example, see p.5v or p.12v for the genealogy of Adam Kleinhardt), 2) Notes were added later in August 1870 and September 1873 (see p.5v), 3) Corrections were made at the Breakwater Convict Station on 10 July 1866 (see p.57), 4) This story is found in Book I
