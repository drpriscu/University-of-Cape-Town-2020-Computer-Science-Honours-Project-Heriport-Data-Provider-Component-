Baboons and quagga are people

Title

Baboons and quagga are people

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

About the Baboons and the Quagga, who were formerly people when those who are now people were not alive. Even though they are animals their organs still smell like they did when they were people.
