Regarding the living again of male ostriches

Title

Regarding the living again of male ostriches

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The male Ostrich believed to possess the power of returning once to life. (L VIII.31. 8750 rev.-8752 rev.). The male ostrich is the one who returns to life after it has died and is called ||khabbi-ssin. The hen ostrich really dies and does not return to life.
