||xuobbeten and the lion

Title

||xuobbeten and the lion

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

||xuobbeten is carried away by the lion while she sleeps. It bites her head and kills her. It puts her in its mouth and carries her down to the water. The people name the water '||xuobbeten' because it has no bushes.
