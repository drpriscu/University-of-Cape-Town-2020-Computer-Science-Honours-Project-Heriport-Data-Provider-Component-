Xurri ko killed by a lion

Title

Xurri ko killed by a lion

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The death of a relative of the narrator from the bites of a lion, is told in the Katkop dialect by !kweiten ta //ken (L VI.-2. 4010-4013). !kweiten ta ||ken's father and a man called Xurri ko were out hunting quagga when a lion caught Xurri ko and bit him in the leg. Xurri ko died from the severity of his wounds.
