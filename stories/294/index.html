The monster ||khwai-hemm’s speech to the Mantis and the Mantis's reply

Title

The monster ||khwai-hemm’s speech to the Mantis and the Mantis's reply

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The monster ||khwai-hemm's speech and |kaggen's (the Mantis's) reply, in which |kaggen speaks of the monster devouring |kaggen's things and the Porcupine's things, without waiting for the food that |kaggen will give him.

Comments

1) See also The Mantis takes away the Tick's sheep (including Porcupine's speech concerning the coming of ||khwai-hemm), 2) This story is found in Book II-34
