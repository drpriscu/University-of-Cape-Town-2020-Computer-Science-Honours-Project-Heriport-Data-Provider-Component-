Personal history of Jan Plat (he leaves Calvinia with Dia!kwain)

Title

Personal history of Jan Plat (he leaves Calvinia with Dia!kwain)

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

An account of Jan Plat's movements with his travelling companion, Dia!kwain, and his meeting with his wife, who was keeping sheep in Louriet. A further account of Jan Plat's travels with Dia!kwain and the farmer Louw, whose sheep Dia!kwain had been keeping. Jan Plat states that Dia!kwain went with Louw to Kenhardt and then to the Free State.

Comments

1) p.6046e v: Lloyd does not find Dia!kwain's journey to the Free State improbable as he had told her that he thought that his sons might be there, 2) This story is found in Book VII-1
