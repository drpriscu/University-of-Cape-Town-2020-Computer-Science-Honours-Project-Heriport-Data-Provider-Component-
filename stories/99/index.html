The Blue Crane and the girls (including the Blue Crane's speech)

Title

The Blue Crane and the girls (including the Blue Crane's speech)

Collection

Wilhelm Bleek notebooks

Summary

The Blue Crane says to the young girls that she would like to go with them to sit under the great thorn tree where they might kill the lice that are biting her head. Comments on the Blue Crane's speech follow.

Comments

1) This story shows the speech of the Blue Crane, which is the most simple kind of animal speech. The Blue Crane is |kaggen's sister. Special speech is attributed to different animals as well as the Moon. In |xam speech, the way that the being speaks is imitated by movements of the mouth and by clicks, 2) This story is found in Book XXIV
