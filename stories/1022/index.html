|xue becomes !naxane

Title

|xue becomes !naxane

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

|xue becomes !naxane when the sun rises, and in this form the birds eat him. At sunset |xue returns to his own form, and lies down to sleep. The next day at sunrise |xue becomes a larger kind of tree !naxane, but at sunset he becomes |xue again.

Comments

1) !nanni heard this story from his paternal grandfather (Karu); there is a picture by !nanni of the same date (see UCTLB_ D _!n004) that accompanies this story, 2) This story is found in Book XI & XII-5
