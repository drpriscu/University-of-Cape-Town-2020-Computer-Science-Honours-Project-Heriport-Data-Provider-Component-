Names of stars

Title

Names of stars

Collection

Wilhelm Bleek notebooks

Summary

The names of a number of Stars have been ascertained with the kind help of Mr. Maclear. Besides a separate alphabetical index to the Stars, the names of some are given in a supplementary list (B XXV. 2345–2347 and 2354). |xam names of stars and constellations.

Comments

1) p.2345: the names of various white men, 2) p.2355: (17 March 1874) this page features information on Katkop, including its |xam name and where it is situated (given by ≠kasin), 3) (11 October '73) Proper and |xam names of stars and constellations, 4) This story is found in Book XXV
