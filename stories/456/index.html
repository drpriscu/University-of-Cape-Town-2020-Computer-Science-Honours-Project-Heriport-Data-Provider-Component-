Title

How Xaa-ttin asked (or prayed to) the dead magician (named !nuin-|kui-ten) for rain, which was speedily bestowed

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

A dead Sorcerer, name !nuin|kúïten, asked by the narrator’s father for rain; which speedily came. In the Katkop dialect, by Díä!kwãin (L V.-14. 5068rev.-5078-rev.). Dia!kwain's father, Xaa-ttin, prays to his ancestor, the dead rainmaker !nuin-|kui-ten, for rain.
