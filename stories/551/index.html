Baboons and ||xabbiten ||xabbiten

Title

Baboons and ||xabbiten ||xabbiten

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Baboons plan to attack ||xabbiten ||xabbiten. ||xabbiten ||xabbiten asks them what they are saying and then makes remarks about their ugly appearance and climbs a Krieboom tree. He deceives the baboons by calling out to white farmers as if they were near, because baboons fear white men and their guns.

Comments
