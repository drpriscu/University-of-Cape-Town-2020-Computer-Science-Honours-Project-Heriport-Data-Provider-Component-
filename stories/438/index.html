Why !kweiten ta ||ken received her name

Title

Why !kweiten ta ||ken received her name

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Explanation of various personal names was given, in the Katkop dialect, by Díä!kwãin (L V.-10. 4751-4754, 4758-4777, 18. 5373 rev., 19. 5505 rev. and 5506 rev., 20. 5572 and 5573, 5605-5607). !kweiten ta ||ken is an 'orphan's name'.

Comments

1) !kweiten ta ||ken is spelt !kweten ta ||ken in some of the stories by or relating to her, 2) p.4758v: !kweiten ta ||ken's mother's younger sister's name was Xomm-an (her Dutch name was Anna); Dia!kwain cannot recall her 'little' name; she was 'killed by sorcery', 3) pp.4761v-4764v: see The preparation of tinder from a certain thorn-tree, 4) pp.4765v & 4766v: see The making of a needle from a springbok's foreleg, 5) This story is found in Book V-10
