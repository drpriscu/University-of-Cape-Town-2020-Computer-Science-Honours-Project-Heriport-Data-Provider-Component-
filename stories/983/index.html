Sleeping in ashes

Title

Sleeping in ashes

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

Tamme's father's heart aches so his mother tells his father to sleep in ashes. His father does as she says and becomes white from the ashes.

Comments

1) See also Customs at death and Burial, 2) This story is found in Book XI & XII-3
