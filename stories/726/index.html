Title

|a!karaken killed by a lion

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

A lion sits waiting for the sun to set so he can stalk people. The people lie down to sleep and the lion steals up on them and drags |a!karaken into the grove. The people name the grove '|a!karaken's grove' after |a!karaken who was carried there by the lion.
