Water and wells

Title

Water and wells

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

A description of the making of 'wells' (or !ha) in !nanni and Tamme's country. They say that the water has 'one root', which refers to the well in Mowbray.

Comments

1) This story is found in Book XI & XII-10
