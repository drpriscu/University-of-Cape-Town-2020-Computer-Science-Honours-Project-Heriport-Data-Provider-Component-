Title

Wildcat turns into a lion

Collection

Summary

The Wild Cat believed to possess the power of turning itself into a lion. By /han≠kass’o (L VIII.-23. 8080-8083, and 27. 8399 rev., note). |han≠kass'o's grandfather (Tsatsi) told him that when the wild cat is killed and brought home, it turns into a lion and comes to kill the hunter who killed it.
