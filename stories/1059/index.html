Title

Words and sentences: names of animals given by Tamme at the museum

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

Tamme (and occasionally !nanni) provides Lloyd with the !kun names of animals, birds and insects at the South African Museum.

Comments

1) Inserts on the inside front cover of Book XI & XII-9: pencil and pen script in Lloyd's hand concerning birds and insects, 2) Pages 9682-9757 in this notebook are blank, 3) This is found in Book XI & XII-9
