Dia!kwain plays the bow in a thunderstorm, or, The thunderstorm

Title

Dia!kwain plays the bow in a thunderstorm, or, The thunderstorm

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

An account by Dia!kwain of the time that he played the !goura while the rain fell and caused lightning at night. The rain struck them with lightning and they could not see and his mother tells him that the rain wants to kill them.
