The old woman's song

Title

The old woman's song

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The old woman sings about the Hyena who carried her off, and how she sprang aside when he tried to throw her onto a pointed rock, so that he instead fell onto the pointed rock and was killed.

Comments

1) See also The old woman and the hyena and Old woman's song (2nd version), 2) This story is found in Book I- 2
