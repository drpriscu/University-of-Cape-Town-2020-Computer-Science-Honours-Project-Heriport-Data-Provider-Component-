Sitting in the shade

Title

Sitting in the shade

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The shade not to be sat in, unless it is really summer. In the Katkop dialect, by Díä!kwãin (L V.-15. 5149 rev. and 5150 rev.). Dia!kwain's mother told him what happens if one sits in the shade when the weather is not very hot.

Comments

1) Dia!kwain heard this story from his mother (≠kamme-an), 2) This story is found in Book V-15
