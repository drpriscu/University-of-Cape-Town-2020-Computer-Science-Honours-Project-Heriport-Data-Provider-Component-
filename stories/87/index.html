Old woman's song (2nd version)

Title

Old woman's song (2nd version)

Collection

Wilhelm Bleek notebooks

Summary

The old She-Hyena carries away the old woman who has been left lying alone in her hut.

Comments

1) This song and its accompanying notes (see p.2160v) refer to the custom of geriatricide and the treatment of the old and infirm, 2) This story is found in Book XXIII
