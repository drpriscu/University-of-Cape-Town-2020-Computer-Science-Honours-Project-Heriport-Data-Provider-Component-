Title

Leopard hunting: the fatal adventure of !kwai-!kwa and his companion, and advice about leopard-hunting

Collection

Summary

A man called Gemsbok Leg, or !kwai-!kwa, goes hunting with a companion and they pursue a leopard that seems to disappear, but it has deceived them and is lying in wait. It kills !kwai-!kwa before he can warn his companion, who is also attacked.
