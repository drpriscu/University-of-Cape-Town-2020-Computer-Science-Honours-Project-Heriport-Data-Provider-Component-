Title

Remarks concerning copies of Bushman pictures nos. I-XXIV Collected By Mr H.C. Schunke, and deposited in the Grey Library

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Explanatory remarks concerning copies of Bushmen Paintings and Etchings, collected by Mr. H.C. Schunke and deposited in the Grey Library. Partly in Bushman by and partly in English after |han≠kass'o (L VIII.-1. 6054-6057, 6061-6073). A list of remarks by |han≠kass'o alongside the numbers (I-XXIV) of a collection of copies of paintings found in the Grey Library. These paintings are mostly of animals and rainmakers and their work with the rain.

Comments

1) p.6067v & 6068v: |han≠kass'o's note on 'another !khoa ka ||kerri-ssi !kau'; a note on the !khoa ka ||kerri-ssi !kau that |han≠kass'o saw in a painting copied by a 'Kafir' artist, 2) p.6071v: on a man called Oud Koos, or !ka !kain-i, 3) See also An account of the rain's things or '!khoa ka ||kerri-ssi !kau', 4) This story is found in Book VIII-1
