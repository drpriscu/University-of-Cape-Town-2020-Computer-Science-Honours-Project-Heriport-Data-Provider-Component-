About the lion who carries away and kills a man and the search for the lost man (a story of common life)

Title

About the lion who carries away and kills a man and the search for the lost man (a story of common life)

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

A Bushman killed by a Lion; a search for the missing man; removal of the rest to another place, for fear that the Lion should track them to their huts (L II.-8. 892-907, 9. 908-920).

Comments

1) Date on p.908: 22 November, 2) Reverse of frontispiece (Book II-9): 'End of a story of Bushman life, about a Lion which killed a woman's husband, and how the others removed to another place, for fear the lion should track one of them to their huts', 3) This story is found in Books II-8 and 9
