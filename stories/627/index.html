Title

Mode of eating porcupine

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Different customs concerning the eating of the Porcupine. By /han≠kass’o (L VII.-6. 6590 rev.-6594 rev.). The people who live where the sun sets (the place where Dia!kwain and his people come from) roast and eat the porcupine on the hunting-ground, but |han≠kass'o's people take the porcupine home when there are no springbok.
