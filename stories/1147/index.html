All came out of one hole

Title

All came out of one hole

Collection

Lucy Lloyd Kora notebooks

Summary

Very little of this story has been translated. 'the living things which move about in the world. all came out of one hole': Kafirs, Englishmen, !koranna, Boers, Bastards, Orlams, Bushmen all 'passed out' (through the hole, presumably). The rest is harder to piece together.

Comments

1) Piet Lynx heard this story from his father, 2) p.84 opp: Piet says that there are two kinds of Bastards: those with smooth hair and those with short hair, 3) This story is found in Kora Book I-1
