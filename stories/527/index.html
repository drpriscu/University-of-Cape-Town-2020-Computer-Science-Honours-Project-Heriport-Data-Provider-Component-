Title

Explanation of Mr G. Stow's pictures: no. 2

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

An explanation of No. 2 of Mr G.W. Stow’s collection of copies of Bushman Paintings was given, in the Katkop dialect by Díä!kwãin (L V.-22. 5739-5742). The painting features figures (numbered) and animals. Díä!kwãin describes some of the hunting methods of the |xam.
