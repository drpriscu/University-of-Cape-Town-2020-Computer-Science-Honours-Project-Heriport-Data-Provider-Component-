Title

The new maiden who threw !huin into the sky; which became stars, and the wood ashes which were upon it, the Milky Way

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

A Girl being angry with her mother, because the latter asked her for a certain kind of food which she had put to roast in the fire, threw it with the wood–ashes that were upon it, into the air. The food was changed into stars, and the ashes became the Milky Way. By |han≠kass’o (L VIII.-10. 6879-6884). The Milky Way resembles the wood ash and the stars resemble the !huin roots in colour and shape.
