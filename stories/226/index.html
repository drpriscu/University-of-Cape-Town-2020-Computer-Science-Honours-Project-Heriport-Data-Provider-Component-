A discussion on the respective understanding and foolishnesses of various animals and some of their doings

Title

A discussion on the respective understanding and foolishnesses of various animals and some of their doings

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Some animals have' thoughts' (or 'understanding') and can talk as people do. According to ||kabbo (see p.891v), some animals stand upright like a man does. (Much of this story has not been translated.) ... a discourse upon the degrees of understanding possessed by various animals (L II.-8. 882-892).

Comments

1) Date on p.890: 21 November, 2) This story is found in Book II-8
