The hartebeest resembles the Mantis

Title

The hartebeest resembles the Mantis

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The hartebeest's head resembles that of |kaggen (the Mantis), so it feels itself to be 'Mantis's thing'. Some remarkable superstitions concerning the Hartebeest, whose head is said to resemble that of the Mantis; and also a curious charm made from the foot of the Hartebeest, and used by Bushman women for their children as a protection against the Mantis (L V.-6. 4414 rev.-4418 rev.).
