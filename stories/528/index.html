The rainmaker ||kunn

Title

The rainmaker ||kunn

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The Rain Sorcerer, ||kunn. In the Katkop dialect, by Díä!kwãin (L V.-22. 5743-5754). ||kunn says that the people have spoken harshly to him, as if he really owned the rain. They scold him when it does not fall, but forget that they asked him for it when the rain does fall.
