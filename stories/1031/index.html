Further changes of |xue

Title

Further changes of |xue

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

|xue becomes a lizard, a dui, an Omuherero, a shao tree, a Makoba and !naxane, as the sun sets and rises. At one point |xue cries out and his wife takes hold of him. She gives her husband water and he dies, becoming a little bird. |xue's wife calls her mother and father to come and look at |xue, who becomes a little black ant. |xue's father-in-law seizes him and tries to burn him with fire. |xue leaps over the fire and his father-in-law chases him. |xue bites his father-in-law, who slaps him.

Comments

1) p.9380v: (27 March 1880); !nanni heard this story from his paternal grandfather (Karu), 2) p.9381v: the shao tree; a description of a man in a picture Lloyd showed to the boys; about !naxane trees, ground !naxane, and !naxane fruit, 3) p.9383v: other things that |xue becomes (a cat, a fly, great water and grass), 4) This story is found in Book XI & XII-5
