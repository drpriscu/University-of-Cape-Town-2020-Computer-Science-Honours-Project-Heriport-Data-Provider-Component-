!nana-an: the custom of calling to the wounded springbok

Title

!nana-an: the custom of calling to the wounded springbok

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The hunters call to make a wounded springbok die (or 'lie down') quickly (!nana-an). They call out differently for gemsbok and ostriches.
