Stoffel's grandmother

Title

Stoffel's grandmother

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The narrator’s grandmother restored to life. Related by /a!kúnta. (L I.-2. 133) |a!kunta's (Stoffel's) grandmother (his 'oud mamma') is dead. The people come back to look (to 'smell') for her but she has gone away. She has stood up and walked away. The people set her things down and sleep in the darkness, and get up the next day.
