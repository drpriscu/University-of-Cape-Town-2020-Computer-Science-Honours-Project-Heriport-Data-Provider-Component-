The man who sought refuge from the rain in a cave, and found a lion there before him

Title

The man who sought refuge from the rain in a cave, and found a lion there before him

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The lion made the rain in order to confuse the man so he wouldn't know where his house was and the lion could catch him. The lion made it rain and waited in a cave for the man who sought refuge there.

Comments

1) p.4890 opp: this story was told to Dia!kwain by his paternal grandfather (!ku-gen-ddi, or Oud Klaas), 2) p.4905v: a note that the following pages (pp.4906-4926) were translated at Charlottenburg, 3) This story is found in Book V-12
