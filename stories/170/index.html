Lion and giraffe

Title

Lion and giraffe

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Lions and Giraffes, in two versions. (B1. 323-323; L II-1. 255-257) The lion catches the giraffe and leaps on its neck. He springs down because it is very far and falls to the ground and dies. The giraffe also falls to the ground dead, on top of the lion.

Comments

1) Note on the inside of the back cover of this notebook: 'Finished correcting Sept 15/71 from Jantje Toorm himself', 2) This story is found in Book II-1
