The girl of the Early Race who killed the children of the Rain

Title

The girl of the Early Race who killed the children of the Rain

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The Girl (of the Early Race) who killed the Children of the Rain, bringing, thereby, severe punishment upon herself and those who lived with her. By /han≠kass’o (L VIII.-17. 7473-7519). A girl kills and eats a Water child, tidying up before her mother comes home. This happens a number of times, and eventually the Rain becomes angry and makes a whirlwind gather the hut and the girl into the pond, where she becomes a frog.
