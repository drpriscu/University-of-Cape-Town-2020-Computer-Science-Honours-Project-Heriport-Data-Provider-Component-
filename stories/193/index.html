Title

The cutting and piercing of parts of the body

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Cutting off the top of the little finger, and piercing ears and nose. (L II.-2. 357-361.) Describing the cutting of the fingertips, hands and arms of male and female children, and the piercing of the nose and ears of adult men and women.

Comments

1) p.356v: a note describing which parts of which fingers are cut, 2) This story is found in Book II-2
