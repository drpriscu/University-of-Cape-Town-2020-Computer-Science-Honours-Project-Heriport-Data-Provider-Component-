Title

The power of cutting possessed by the reed and quartz

Summary

Stone-knives. By /han≠kass'o (L VIII.-27. 8434). Reed and stone used for cutting purposes. (L VIII.-26. 8313 and 8314. The power of cutting possessed by a reed and by quartz. (L. VII.-21. 7826 rev. and 7827 rev., note). The reed is sharp and can cut and hurt people. Quartz also cuts people deeply.
