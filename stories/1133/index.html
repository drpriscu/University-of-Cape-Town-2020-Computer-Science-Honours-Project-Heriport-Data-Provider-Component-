Words and sentences: given by Griet

Title

Words and sentences: given by Griet

Collection

Lucy Lloyd Kora notebooks

Summary

Griet gives words in her language to Lloyd in Mowbray, mostly concerning parts of the body and animals.

Comments

1) p.1 opp: 'Makas is, Piet says, Griet's other name', and another genealogical note, 2) pp.2 opp-4 opp have one word notes on them, some with Jantje's name next to them, 3) p.5: "Stal" is 'another place where Griet lived', 4) This story is found in Kora Book I-1
