How Korannas and the |xam cut themselves in order to shoot well

Title

How Korannas and the |xam cut themselves in order to shoot well

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Korannas cut themselves in order to shoot well and cut the back of their right wrists when they fight with their fists so that they may strike a man dead. The |xam cut their hand between the thumb and first finger so that they may shoot the game well.
