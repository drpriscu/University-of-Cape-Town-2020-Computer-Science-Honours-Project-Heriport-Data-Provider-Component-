The adventure of |khui- |a with a family of baboons

Title

The adventure of |khui- |a with a family of baboons

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

|khui- |a is out hunting and shoots a mother baboon who is carrying a child. The father baboon goes to find the child and asks |khui- |a to leave it alone and not kill it, as he has already taken the baby's mother from him. |khui- |a is frightened and picks up his things and leaves them alone.
