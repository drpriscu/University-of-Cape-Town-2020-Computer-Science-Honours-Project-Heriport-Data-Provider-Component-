Treatment of the old

Title

Treatment of the old

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

The !kun do not 'throw away' their old people, but keep them with them. The old people take care of the small children when the mothers are off collecting food.

Comments

1) This story is found in Book XI & XII-10

Contributors

!nanni (XI), Tamme (XII)

Date
