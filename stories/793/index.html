Title

A necklace of reed used to cure a little child's cold

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

A reed necklace employed as a remedy for a little child suffering from a cold (L VIII.-21. 7826 and 7827). The women break and burn pieces of reed in the fire.
