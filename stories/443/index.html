Moons' (months) possess their names

Title

Moons' (months) possess their names

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The names for August and September were given by Díä!kwãin, by whom it was also stated that the names for all the months are known to the old people. In the Katkop dialect (L V.-10. 4796).
