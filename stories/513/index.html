Explanation of Mr G. Stow's pictures: 1 (habits of the hunting leopard)

Title

Explanation of Mr G. Stow's pictures: 1 (habits of the hunting leopard)

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Dia!kwain comments on George Stow's copy of a Bushman painting (no. 1) which features figures and various animals (numbered). He describes the doings of the |xam with regard to hunting. Dia!kwain goes on to describe the habits of the hunting leopard.

Comments

1) p.5630v: the other name of the Orange River (Tki), given after sea water (which is cold), 2) p.5638v: includes the name of a family friend of Dia!kwain's mother who died after being injured by a leopard, 3) p.5639v: the |xam names of stones, 4) This story is found in Book V-21
