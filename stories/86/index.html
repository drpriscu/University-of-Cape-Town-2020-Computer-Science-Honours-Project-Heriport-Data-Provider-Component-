Jackal's song (and The song of the Caama Fox)

Title

Jackal's song (and The song of the Caama Fox)

Collection

Wilhelm Bleek notebooks

Summary

The Jackal's song in which it sings of how it avoids being hunted by circling back and crossing the spoor of the dogs that chase it.

Comments

1) The !gwiten is otherwise known as Vulpes Caama or the caama fox (or the jackal), 2) The song of the Caama fox appears on pp.2158v-2159v, 3) This story is found in Book XXIII
