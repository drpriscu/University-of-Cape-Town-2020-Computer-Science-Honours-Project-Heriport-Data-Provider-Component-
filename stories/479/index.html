Title

Dia!kwain explains his mother's 'little name'

Collection

Summary

Explanation of various personal names was given, in the Katkop dialect, by Díä!kwãin (L V.-10. 4751-4754, 4758-4777, 18. 5373 rev., 19. 5505 rev. and 5506 rev., 20. 5572 and 5573, 5605-5607). Accounting for Dia!kwain's mother's 'little' name, which was |ko-an.
