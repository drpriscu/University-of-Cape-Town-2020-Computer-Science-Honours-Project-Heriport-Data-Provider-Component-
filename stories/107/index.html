Title

Words and sentences: names of animals given by Klaas Katkop (≠kasin), ||kabbo (Jantje) and |a!kunta (Stoffel)

Collection

Wilhelm Bleek notebooks

Summary

Names of animals, mainly identified at the Museum (B II. 358 and 359, 366-368, XXV. 2341-2344, 2349 and 2350)...and lastly in the Katkop dialect by #kásin (B XXV. 2381-2384, or 2424-2428). In the same dialect are also some names of animals, explaining the Bushman pictures copied by Mr Schunke. (B XXVII. 2619-2621.) |xam names of animals seen at the South African Museum by ||kabbo and |a!kunta, and also by ≠kasin, some also in Koranna.

Comments

1) Inserts on inside of front cover of Book 25 (XXV) and facing page: names of animals (crossed out) by ≠kasin (Klaas Katkop), dated 1 November 1873 (date of his arrival), 2) This story is found in Book XXV, Book XXVII

Contributors

Date
