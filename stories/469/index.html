Title

A small insect called !ka !karro, said to resemble the Moon, used by Bushmen women to ascertain if the men will bring food home

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

A small insect called !ka !karro resembles and shares the Moon's name. The !ka !karro tells the truth because it 'possesses' game.

Comments

1) pp.5173-5260 were translated in April 1912; except for pp.5206-5209, 2) Date on p.5245: 16 August, 3) See also The Moon, 4) This story is found in Book V-16
