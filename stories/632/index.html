Rainbow (|kaggen and !kwammana)

Title

Rainbow (|kaggen and !kwammana)

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The Mantis and his son–in–law /kwámmana are both to be seen in the rainbow, the Mantis uppermost and /kwámmana underneath. (L VIII.-7. 6600 rev. & 6601 rev.). |kaggen (the Mantis) and |kwammana can be seen in the rainbow and are the same colour as the part they live in. The people call the rainbow by |kwammana's name.

Comments

1) |kwammana is also spelt |kuamman-a in this story, 2) See also Rainbow (|kaggen and |kwammana), 3) This story is found in Book VIII-7
