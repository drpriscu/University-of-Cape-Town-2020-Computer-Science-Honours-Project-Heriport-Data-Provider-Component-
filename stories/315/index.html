The consequences of a woman's smelling fresh Ssho |oa scent

Title

The consequences of a woman's smelling fresh Ssho |oa scent

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

If women smell the Ssho |oa's scent (of a plant that is really new or fresh) they become very ill. The newly-brought Shó-|õä is feared by the women (L II.-36. 3279-3286).

Comments

1) Note on the inside of the cover of Book II-36: pp.3242-3332 were translated in September 1873, but Lloyd 'Began to enter from p.3261 Aug. 1st 1911.', 2) See also About Ssho |oa: where to be found and An ignorant man digs up Ssho |oa and the consequences of his actions and How women fear the new Ssho |oa which has just been brought home and Ssho |oa and What the man does and says to the new Ssho |oa so that it may know him, 3) This story is found in Book II-36
