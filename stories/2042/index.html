Dorothea Bleek - Book BC151_A3_018

Title

Dorothea Bleek - Book BC151_A3_018

Collection

Dorothea Bleek notebooks

Summary

Naron notebook (volume VII). Descriptions of daily life, contemporary life and personal history. Much about sorcery (doctors and old people), healing, cutting practises, games (eg the 'war game'), dancing and magic animals (such as baboons and lions); artefacts and dress, food hunted and eaten, relations with inhabitants of the region; marriage, birth, name-giving and other family life; and naming stars and asking stars for food, as well as rain and related animals such as the mantis. References to the coming of Christianity and associated beliefs.
