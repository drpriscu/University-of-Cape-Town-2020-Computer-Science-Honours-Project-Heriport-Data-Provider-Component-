The little elephant

Title

The little elephant

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

A story in which an elephant father and mother dig out a pit for a little elephant near to a !goa tree. The mother elephant puts the little elephant in the pit and fills the pit with earth. At sunset the elephant mother awakes and lifts the little elephant out of the pit. The two of them run down to the water and the mother puts the little elephant into the water.

Comments

1) See also The little elephant (which fell into the game pit but did not die), 2) This story is found in Book XI & XII-4

!nanni (XI), Tamme (XII)
