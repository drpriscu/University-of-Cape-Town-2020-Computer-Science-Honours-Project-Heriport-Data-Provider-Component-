Grasshoppers

Title

Grasshoppers

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

The grasshopper of !nanni's and Tamme's country (called the !gau) eats small grass flowers. The little grasshopper eats the blades of the grass and the large grasshopper eats the stems.

Comments

1) 9380v: see Further changes of |xue, 2) This story is found in Book XI & XII-5
