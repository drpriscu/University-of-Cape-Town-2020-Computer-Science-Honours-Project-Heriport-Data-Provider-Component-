Title

Song of the !kan ||ka ||karashe

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

!nanni tells the song of the !kan ||ka ||karashe. This is a small animal eaten by the !kun which scratches out food from the ground called ||kauru.
