Title

A few words, names, etc., obtained from Blaitje Snell and Daoud Moos (chiefly the former); from Stuurman's, and Thier Fontein

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Names, words and sentences given by Blaitje Snell and Daoud Moos from Stuurman's and Thierfontein, the names of their relations and where they come from. Words in the dialect of Stuurmansfontein, in the Kareebergen (B XXIV. 2261; L II.-35. 3160-3164).

Comments

1) p.3159v: the location of Thierfontein and Stuurmansfontein (near or in the Kareebergen), 2) See also Words and sentences: Kareebergen Stuurmansfontein dialect, 3) This story is found in Book II-35
