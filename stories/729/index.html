Porcupine hunting

Title

Porcupine hunting

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

An account of the hunting of porcupine and the practices that ensure its success (such as the treatment of their bones). The hunter is 'worked on' by the women before he goes to seek porcupine. The porcupine goes about only at night and remains inside its hole when the place is bright.
