Different peoples and Bushmen in !nanni and Tamme's country

Title

Different peoples and Bushmen in !nanni and Tamme's country

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

Information about various different peoples and other groups of !kun (or !ku) found in !nanni and Tamme's country. There is a list of these groups of peoples and their differences and similarities.

Comments

1) See also The song of the !korro-ssin !ku and A song of the |kam-ssin !ku (or Sun Bushmen) and Tamme's family and their understanding of the languages spoken in their country, 2) p.9177v: see Food eaten by the !kun (respect for the wild cat); how eating it causes pain in the head, 3) 9181v: see A song of the |kam-ssin !ku (or Sun Bushmen), 4) This story is found in Book XI & XII-3
