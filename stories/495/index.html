Dia!kwain's explanation of the name which his mother gave him

Title

Dia!kwain's explanation of the name which his mother gave him

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Explanation of various personal names was given, in the Katkop dialect, by Díä!kwãin (L V.-10. 4751-4754, 4758-4777, 18. 5373 rev., 19. 5505 rev. and 5506 rev., 20. 5572 and 5573, 5605-5607). Dia!kwain's explanation of the name his mother gave him after her family was roughly beaten by people who she thought showed no pity.
