How the approach of a commando is foretold by the mist

Title

How the approach of a commando is foretold by the mist

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The near approach of a 'Commando' is heralded by a mist. In the Katkop dialect, by Díä!kwãin (L V.-16. 5199-5205). The |xam can tell when a commando is coming to attack them by the mist that comes in the morning.

Comments

1) This story was told to Dia!kwain by his father (Xaa-ttin), 2) pp.5173-5260 were translated in April 1912; except for pp.5206-5209, 3) This story is found in Book V-16
