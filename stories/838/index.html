Title

The story of |gwai (who killed his sister-in-law, and was killed by his brother-in-law)

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

|gwai (a person who was formerly a beast of prey and a man of the Early Race) kills his sister-in-law and his brother-in-law is angry with him and seeks revenge (the title implies that |gwai is caught and killed but it does not happen in the story).
