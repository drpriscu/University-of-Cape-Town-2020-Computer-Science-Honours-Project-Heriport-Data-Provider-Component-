Title

Food eaten by the !kun

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

The names and descriptions of some plants and animals eaten in !nanni and Tamme's country. It includes notes on whether the food is eaten raw or cooked, and what food is found in the Damara's country.
