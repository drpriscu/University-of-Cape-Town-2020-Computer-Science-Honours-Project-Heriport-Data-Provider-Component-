Words and sentences: given by Piet Lynx

Title

Words and sentences: given by Piet Lynx

Collection

Lucy Lloyd Kora notebooks

Summary

Words and sentences about animals, family members, body parts and plants given by Piet Lynx (some at the Museum in Cape Town), with some additional information from !nanni and Tamme.

Comments

1) p.95 opp: there are two additional columns of words in pencil, headed '!nanni' and 'Tamme', on this page, 2) There are notes in pencil by Lucy Lloyd and Louis Maingard throughout these pages, 3) p.96 opp: a note by Piet on the real "Tier", 4) p. 98 opp: 'Piet says that they call the Klip-Springer the "BergDuiker"', 5) p.107 opp: there are two columns on this page headed "Bushman" and "Koms Bushman", with four entries below them, 6) p.109 opp: note on a root that is plentiful in Piet's land which is eaten by the porcupine, and when it is time to hunt quagga, 7) p.110: date in the middle of the page, below the heading 'Burial', is '3 Oct.', 8) this story is found in Kora Book I-2

Contributors

Piet Lynx, !nanni, Tamme

Date

6 Sept./79 - 25 Oct.

Categories

Words and sentences, Plants and animals
