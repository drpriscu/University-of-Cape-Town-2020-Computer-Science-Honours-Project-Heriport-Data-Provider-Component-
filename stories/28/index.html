Leopards and jackals

Title

Leopards and jackals

Collection

Wilhelm Bleek notebooks

Summary

The Jackal howls and begs the Leopard for springbok flesh for it to eat. The Jackal angers the Leopard which then kills it and places its body in a bush.

1) This story is found in Book II
