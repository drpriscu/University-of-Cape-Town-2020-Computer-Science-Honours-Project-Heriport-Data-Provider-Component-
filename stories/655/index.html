Kinds of rain

Title

Kinds of rain

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Different kinds of rain. The children to hide from rain that is likely to be strong, for fear of the lightning. (L VIII.-9. 6813 rev.-6815 rev.). Liquid rain falls gently, a big rain falls in hard streams, another rain 'makes lightening' and makes the place black.
