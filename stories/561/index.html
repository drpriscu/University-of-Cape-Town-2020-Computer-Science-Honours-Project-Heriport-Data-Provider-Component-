Title

Jan Plat's aunt Natta sang the ≠gebbi-ggu

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Jan Plat's aunt Natta sang and taught the people the singing of the ≠gebbi-ggu. She could imitate (by singing) a ewe's call to its young as well as the call of a partridge, using different parts of her throat.
