The !kwana thorn tree

Title

The !kwana thorn tree

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Names of Trees and Plants, and other information regarding them, given partly in Bushman by and partly in English after /han≠kass’o (L VIII.-14. 7219 rev. and 7220 rev., note, 18. 7552 rev.-7754 rev., 21. 7830-7833, 7835, 7847, 23. 8061 rev., 8062 rev. and 8063 rev.,8073 rev., 29. 8601 rev., 8606 rev., 8613 rev., 30. 8637 rev., 31. 8728 rev., 8790, 8791, 8789 rev.). The !kwana thorn tree (and bush) scratches and wounds people who do not wear trousers when they hunt porcupine in the bushes at night.
