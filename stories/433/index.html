The Bushman doctress and sorceress Ttanno !khauken

Title

The Bushman doctress and sorceress Ttanno !khauken

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

An old sorceress (a relative who befriended the narrator’s family), her power of turning herself into a lioness, her pet springbok, etc. By Dia!kwãin (L V.-10. 4707-4743). Dia!kwain's aunt, the sorceress Ttanno !khauken, changes herself into a lioness to see what her people are doing and to take care of them. Dia!kwain's father kills her short-horned springbok, but she is not angry even though it is not a food springbok, but a tame one (it was her 'heart's springbok').
