Springbok possess magic arrows

Title

Springbok possess magic arrows

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The springbok possess magic or invisible arrows which make people ill. Little children are not allowed to play on the springbok's skin, because the springbok gets under the people's flesh and makes them ill.
