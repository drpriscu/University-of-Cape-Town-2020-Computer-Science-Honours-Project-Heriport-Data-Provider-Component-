Men who hunted lions with bones

Title

Men who hunted lions with bones

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Two brothers succesfully hunt Lions with bones, until the younger brother goes out hunting alone and is killed. The elder brother waits at home for his younger brother and the Lions come and kill him.
