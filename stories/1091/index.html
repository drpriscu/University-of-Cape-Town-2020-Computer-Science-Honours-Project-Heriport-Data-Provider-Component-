Title

Hu'-we (is |xue, and is a Bushman)

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

!nanni's grandfather Karu told him about Hu'-we, who is a !kun. 'Hu'-we' is what the !kun call a person who works many things, and the people also call him |xue.
