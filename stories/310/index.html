What |kaggen does when an eland has been shot

Title

What |kaggen does when an eland has been shot

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

When an eland has been shot, |kaggen (the Mantis) buzzes in the hunters' ears, stands on their eyebrows and looks in their quivers to find the 'offending' arrow. He looks at the bowstrings to see if they have been used.
