Dorothea Bleek - Book BC151_A3_017

Title

Dorothea Bleek - Book BC151_A3_017

Collection

Dorothea Bleek notebooks

Summary

Naron notebook (volume V) Detailed description of dances, ghosts and spirits, burial of the dead, sorcery and magic arrows, addressing the rain, and related practices. Much personal and local history, names and details about the past and what the old people used to teach. References to recordings. Descriptions of hunting and cooking, arrow-making, cutting and healing, trading, smoking and eating, male and female practises, songs & different types of dances. Mention of creatures/beings/spirits and gives their names and beliefs related to them (||gauwa, |gone, !xuba, Hisesha).
