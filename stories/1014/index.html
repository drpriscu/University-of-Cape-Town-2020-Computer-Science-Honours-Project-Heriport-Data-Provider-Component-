Title

Tamme does not tell about |xue

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

Tamme's father told him about |xue, as !nanni's grandfather Karu had told him. !nanni talks about |xue but Tamme does not. Tamme gives his mother's father's name.
