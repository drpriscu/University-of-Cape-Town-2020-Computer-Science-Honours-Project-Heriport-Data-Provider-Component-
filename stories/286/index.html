The treatment of the 'growing' girl

Title

The treatment of the 'growing' girl

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

||kabbo describes the seclusion of the 'growing' (or menstruating) girl in a hut with a tiny door by her mother: she has to lookeep her eyes on the ground when she leaves the hut and cannot go far. When she is 'grown' she is allowed to look about and leave the hut and walk about like other women.

Comments

1) See The girl who made the Milky Way, by throwing ashes into the sky, 2) This story is found in Book II-28
