Title

Moon and stars: an incantation

Collection

Wilhelm Bleek notebooks

Summary

Various statements are given with regard to the nature and movements of the celestial bodies. The first of these treats of the Moon and Stars, ending with the “Bushman–rice” Star Canopus (B I. 291–294... An incantation to the Moon and stars.
