When a good-looking person is ill

Title

When a good-looking person is ill

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

The handsome child, who is ill, is not to be sent to fetch water. By /han≠kass’o (L VIII.-15. 7296-7298). When a 'good-looking' or handsome person is ill he is taken care of because of his appearance.

Comments

1) |han≠kass'o heard this story from his mother (|xabbi-an), 2) Sorcerers 'seize' good-looking people, making them ill: see also Sorcerers are like lions, 3) This story is found in Book VIII-15
