Burial; also avenging a death

Title

Burial; also avenging a death

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

When one of the !kun dies, the people dig a grave and put the dead person into it. The body is covered in earth and the grave is filled in. A tree is cut down and laid over the grave. |uma relates the death of one of his relatives who was killed by an arrow. His people put the relative into the earth and the women cried for him. If a stranger is killed they do not bury him. The account includes mention of how tobacco is shared with a stranger. If the man returns with none to share he is killed.
