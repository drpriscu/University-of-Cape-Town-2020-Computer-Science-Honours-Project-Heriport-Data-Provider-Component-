Title

Words and sentences: parts of the body

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Words in the Katkop dialect, from #kásin (B XXV. 2355-2360), from !kwéiten ta //ken (L VI.-1. 3882, 3859-3897), and from Dia!kwãin (L V.-6. 4434 and 4435, 8. 4615 rev., 9. 4653 rev., etc.). A list of |xam names for parts of the body, including the 'great throat artery', head and neck bones, and sinews.
