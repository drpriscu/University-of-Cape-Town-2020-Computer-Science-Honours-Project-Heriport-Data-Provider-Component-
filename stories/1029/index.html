Hai ||umm

Title

Hai ||umm

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

The Hai-||umm resemble the !kun. They speak the same language as the Berg Damaras and also speak !kun and the !kun understand their language. The Hai-||umm's country is near that of the !kun. The !kun buy something called sa from them.
