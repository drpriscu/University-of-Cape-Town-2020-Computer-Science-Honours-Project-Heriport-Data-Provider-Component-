Sun and Moon story

Title

Sun and Moon story

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

A continuation of the The Sun pierces the full Moon with his knife, in which the Moon asks the Sun to leave his /kuirri (piece of neck sinew) so that the children can still see him come. The Sun consents, letting go of him (putting his knife back into his bag).

Comments

1) This story is a continuation of The Sun pierces the full Moon with his knife, p.292, 2) Additions have been made by Dia!kwain on the verso pages of the story, 3) This story is found in Book II-4
