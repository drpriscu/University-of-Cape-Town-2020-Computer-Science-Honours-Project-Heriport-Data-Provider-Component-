How to get rid of the evil influence of bad dreams

Title

How to get rid of the evil influence of bad dreams

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Mode of getting rid of the evil influence of bad dreams. In the Katkop dialect, by Díä!kwãin (L V.-15. 5160 rev.-5163 rev.). What Dia!kwain's mother did when she wished to get rid of the evil influence of bad dreams.

Comments

1) It seems the people address the Moon when they have bad dreams: it is addressed as 'Rider yonder' in the previous story (of a prayer to the Moon) as well as in this instance, 2) See also A prayer addressed to the Moon, and another version of the Moon and Hare story, 3) This story is found in Book V-15
