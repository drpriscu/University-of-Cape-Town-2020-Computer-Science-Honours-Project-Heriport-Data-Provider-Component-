Further details of Men who hunted lions with bones

Title

Further details of Men who hunted lions with bones

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

(Further details of the story of the men who hunted with bones.) The elder brother waits at home for his younger brother to return from hunting Lions. He sees the Lion's eyes shining and thinks they are stars and they approach and seize him. There follows a discussion of the bones the brothers made into knobkerries in order to hunt Lions.

Comments

1) See also Men who hunted lions with bones, 2) p.7572v: the elder brother's song; the people of the Early Race call the lion ||kuamma, 3) This story is found in Book VIII-18
