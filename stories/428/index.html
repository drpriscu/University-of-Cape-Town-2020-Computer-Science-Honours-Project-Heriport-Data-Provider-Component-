Title

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

[A] note explaining the curious name of the son of a sorceress.(L V.-9. 4652 and 4651 rev.). The naming of the Bushman Ssu-!kui-ten-tta (or Snore-White-Lying or 'Witbooi'). His mother 'snored' people without a kaross.

Comments

1) See p.4669v for more details about Ssu-!kui-ten-tta (Snore-White-Lying or 'Witbooy'), who died at the Breakwater and who was one of Dia!kwain's 'mates', 2) See also What the springbok and the gemsbok did when they knew that Dia!kwain's wife would die (it features Ssu-!kui-ten-tta), 3) This story is found in Book V-9
