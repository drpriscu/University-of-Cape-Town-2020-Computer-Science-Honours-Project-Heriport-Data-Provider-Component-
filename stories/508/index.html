How 'Mansse' obtained the name of !kau ||hoan

Title

How 'Mansse' obtained the name of !kau ||hoan

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Explanation of various personal names was given, in the Katkop dialect, by Díä!kwãin (L V.-10. 4751-4754, 4758-4777, 18. 5373 rev., 19. 5505 rev. and 5506 rev., 20. 5572 and 5573, 5605-5607). An account of how Dia!kwain's brother-in-law, Mansse, was given the nickname of !kau |ho an, or 'Stone-knee', after he tripped and fell and cut his knee on a stone.

Comments

1) p.5604v: Mansse married Dia!kwain's sister, |a-kkumm, 2) This story is found in Book V-20
