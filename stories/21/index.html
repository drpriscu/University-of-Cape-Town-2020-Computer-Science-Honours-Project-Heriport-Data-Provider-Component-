The |xam and the hyena

Title

The |xam and the hyena

Collection

Wilhelm Bleek notebooks

Summary

Youths hunt and kill hyena and then the people at home make soup out of their flesh. The people sleep after eating.

Comments

1) p.315v: an insert (pasted in) with 'Cape Colony' stamp addressed to 'Dr Bleek, Mowbray', 2) This story is found in Book I
