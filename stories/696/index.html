The death of the !kau (a lizard of the genus Agama)

Title

The death of the !kau (a lizard of the genus Agama)

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

A story of the !khau (the Kochelman, or Agama lizard) in the Early Times when animals were people (7206opp). As he passes through the mountains, they squeeze and break him. The front of his body falls off and becomes !guru |na and his back falls of and becomes |xe-!khwai.
