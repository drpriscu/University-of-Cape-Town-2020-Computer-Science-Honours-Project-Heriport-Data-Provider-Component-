Title

Words and sentences: given by !nanni and Tamme in Mowbray

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

!nanni and Tamme (at Mowbray) contribute words and sentences relating to everyday life in their country. Some words have been given in Herero, but there are also references to other groups of people in the area, such as the Ovaherero and the Makoba (and what they call things).

Comments
