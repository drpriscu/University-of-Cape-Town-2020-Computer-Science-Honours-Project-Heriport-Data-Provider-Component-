Words and sentences

Title

Words and sentences

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Words and sentences given by |a!kunta (Stoffel). Included are copies, translated in January 1871, of some of the material collected in Lloyd's first notebooks. There is a short account of |a!kunta's wife, who waits for him in his house and cannot eat springbok (p.137). There is an incantation or verse about 'Kleine Jackals' (Lloyd says 'The Bushmen do not sing the jackal') and a note on how they are caught in their holes (p.122v).

Comments

1) Date on p.119opp: 19 November 1870; p.119: 30 July 1871, 2) This story is found in Book I-1 and I-2
