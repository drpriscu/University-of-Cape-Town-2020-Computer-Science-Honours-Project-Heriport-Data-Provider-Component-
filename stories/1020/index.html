||gan-a (spirits or dreams)

Title

||gan-a (spirits or dreams)

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

The !kun call spirits (or dreams) ||gan-a. Other people in their country (namely the Makoba and the Ovaherero) call them different things when they speak of them.

Comments

1) This story is found in Book XI & XII-5
