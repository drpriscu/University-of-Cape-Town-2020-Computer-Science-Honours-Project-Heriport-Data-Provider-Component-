Greetings among the Bushmen (and times of the day)

Title

Greetings among the Bushmen (and times of the day)

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Forms of Salutation used by Bushmen. By /han≠kass’o (L VIII.-32. 8853-8857, 8852 rev.). Men and women as well as the young and the old greet in different ways. There are expressions for saluting one another (called kuanna-i), and greetings made by people who are comfortable or not (and avoid saluting one another).
