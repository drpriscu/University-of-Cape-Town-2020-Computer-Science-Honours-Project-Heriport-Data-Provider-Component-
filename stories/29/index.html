||kabbo (Jantje) in the train

Title

||kabbo (Jantje) in the train

Collection

Wilhelm Bleek notebooks

Summary

||kabbo (Jantje) goes on a journey in a train to Simonstown. He meets a woman who stops him from falling out of the train, as well as a black man and a white man, each of whom he describes. The black man asks him where he comes from and he answers that his place is the 'Bitterpits'.
