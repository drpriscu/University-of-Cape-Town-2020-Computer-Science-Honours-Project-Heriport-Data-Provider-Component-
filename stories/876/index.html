Title

Prayer to a star

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Prayer to the Stars. By /han≠kass’o (L VIII.-28. 8447-8449). Prayer of the narrator’s grandfather to the star Canopus. (L VIII.-28. 8452-8458). People pray to the stars so that they will be able to see game (that their dogs will kill for them) on the hunting-ground at night.

Comments

1) See also What the stars say, 2) p.8446v: |han≠kass'o observed this himself: he heard his maternal grandfather (Tsatsi) talking to the stars, 3) This story is found in Book VIII-28
