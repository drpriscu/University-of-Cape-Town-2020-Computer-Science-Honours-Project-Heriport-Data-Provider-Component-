The escape of |kannan from the Koranna commando

Title

The escape of |kannan from the Koranna commando

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

/kannan, who warned the people in vain of the approach of a Koranna war party, and was the only one who escaped. By /han≠kass’o (L VIII.-26. 8269-8285). A woman called |kannan sees a Koranna commando and returns home to warn her people. An old man insists that she has seen little ostriches and |kannan says she knows she has seen real people, but the people do not believe her. The Korannas attack them and |kannan alone survives the slaughter of her people.
