The song of the !ke tsa'ba (a bird)

Title

The song of the !ke tsa'ba (a bird)

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

The song of the !ke tsa'ba which is a large black bird. It is respected and not eaten. It eats a root called the djon.
