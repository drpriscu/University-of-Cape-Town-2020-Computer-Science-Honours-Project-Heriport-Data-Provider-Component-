End of Moon and Hare story

Title

End of Moon and Hare story

Collection

Lucy Lloyd !kun notebooks

Summary

!nanni concludes the legend of the Hare and the Moon in which the Hare has told the Moon that the people and others must really die.

Comments

1) p.10 027 opp: !nanni heard this from his paternal grandfather (Karu), 2) This story is a continuation of another, see The Moon and the Hare and |xue (Tamme) in Books XI & XII-8, 3) See also Moon and Sun and Hares and Story of the Moon and the Hare: a version at first by ≠kasin and then by Dia!kwain (in |xam Book IV-4) and The Moon and the little Hare (in |xam Book II-6), 4) This story is found in Book XI
