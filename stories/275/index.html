Rainmaking: another story of it

Title

Rainmaking: another story of it

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

Rain-making: told to //kábbo by a Rain-maker who was his foster-father. (L II.-25. 2264-2319 1/2.) About the rainmaker, parts of the rain, the rain and seasons and clouds and the wind. Also describes the doings of springbok and people, and different types of rain and water. The verso pages contain additional information about things relating to the rain and rainmaking.

Comments

1) Note on the inside of the front cover (Book II-25) that some parts of the story were translated in 1899, 2) Date on p.2271: 12 December; p.2310: 23 July, 3) pp.2264opp & 2264v: see The death of |kannu the Rain's man who was ||kabbo's person, 4) See also Rainmaking and The story of the old man who makes rain and Rainmaking (a version by Dia!kwain) and What happened when the thong (with which they were pulling at the Water's Bull) broke or Kko-kkoro's story, 5) This story is found in Book II-25
