Words and sentences (dictionary and genealogies)

Title

Words and sentences (dictionary and genealogies)

Collection

Wilhelm Bleek notebooks

Summary

Words and sentences, principally concerning daily life, including a lion killing a man and going into the heavens (289).

Comments

1) This story contains many notes on the verso pages concerning names of |xam, their genealogies, the names of places where they come from and the names of different groups of |xam (see pp.114v, 221v and 270v), 2) p.305v: the |xam name for a lion used by children, 3) p.315v: insert: a letter addressed to 'Dr Bleek Mowbray' concerning |xam vocabulary, 4) Notes were added later throughout October 1871 (see pp.250v-255v), 5) Words and sentences by ||kabbo (Jantje) were added throughout February 1871 (see p.268), 6) This story is found in Book I and Book II

Contributors

|a!kunta (Stoffel) (I), ||kabbo (Jantje) (II)

Date

September-December 1870, January-May 1871

Categories

Words and sentences

Keywords
