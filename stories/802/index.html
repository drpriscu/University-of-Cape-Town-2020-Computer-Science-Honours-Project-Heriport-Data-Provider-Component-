Title

Notes on rock painting copy no. 3

Collection

Lucy Lloyd |xam notebooks

Summary

|han≠kass'o describes a group of people who are fighting each other (as they appear in a copy of rock painting no. 3). A man on horseback has stabbed another. The people ask the other people for tobacco and fear the white men.
